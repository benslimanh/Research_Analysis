## ğŸ“Š Key Findings

### 1. Performance (Returns)
* **The Islamic Fund (SPUS)** outperformed the traditional market benchmark.
* *Reasoning:* This is largely driven by the exclusion of financial institutions and heavy weighting in high-growth technology stocks (e.g., NVIDIA, Apple, Microsoft).

### 2. Risk Analysis (Volatility)
* **Volatility:** SPUS demonstrated slightly **higher volatility** compared to SPY.
* **Why?** The exclusion of the banking sector (which typically acts as a stabilizer) creates a **"Sector Allocation Bias"** towards the Technology sector, which is inherently more volatile.
